Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our DA tracking and lead generation service.

DAConnect monitors development applications (DAs) across multiple council areas in real-time. Our system identifies new applications that match your business criteria, such as location, project type, and budget. You'll receive instant notifications about relevant opportunities, giving you a head start to reach out to potential clients before your competitors do.

We track a wide range of development applications including new residential builds, renovations, extensions, commercial developments, industrial projects, and more. Our system categorizes each application by type, value, and scope, making it easy for you to find the projects that best match your business services.

Our system updates multiple times daily to ensure you have access to the most current development applications. We monitor council websites and planning portals in real-time, and typically have new applications in our system within hours of them being lodged with the council.
