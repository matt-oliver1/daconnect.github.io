DAconnect Compliance & Privacy Statement

Effective Date: March 2026

DAconnect operates as an independent data intelligence and business connection platform built upon publicly available information.

We are committed to operating responsibly, transparently, and in accordance with applicable Australian laws, including the Spam Act 2003 (Cth) and the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

1. Communications Compliance

DAconnect communications:

  • Are sent in a commercial context relevant to development activity
  • Clearly identify DAconnect as the sender
  • Contain a functional and free unsubscribe facility
  • Are limited in frequency and purpose
  • Are based solely on publicly available records and public business information

For development applicants, communications are limited to contextual notifications relating to specific publicly listed applications. We do not enrol recipients into ongoing marketing without consent.

DAconnect relies on inferred consent where appropriate in commercial (B2B) contexts and applies internal capacity assessment processes before issuing communications.

All unsubscribe requests are processed promptly and in accordance with statutory requirements.

DAconnect is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any council, government department, or regulatory authority.

2. Privacy & Data Handling

DAconnect handles personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

Collection of Information

DAconnect collects information that is lawfully and publicly available through:

  • Public planning registers
  • Public business websites
  • Public business social media pages

Information collected may include names, business details, contact email addresses, phone numbers, and development application details.

Purpose of Collection

Information is collected solely for the purpose of:

  • Providing development-related business intelligence
  • Connecting development applicants with relevant local service providers
  • Delivering contextual commercial communications

DAconnect does not sell personal information.

Data Security

DAconnect takes reasonable steps to protect information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure.

Access, Correction & Deletion

Individuals may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information by contacting:

compliance@daconnect.com.au

08 7228 6805

All requests are reviewed promptly and respectfully.

Governance Commitment

DAconnect is committed to operating as a responsible and structured national data intelligence platform. We maintain internal compliance systems, suppression controls, and complaint handling procedures to ensure our operations remain lawful, transparent, and accountable.

If you have any concerns regarding a communication received from DAconnect, we encourage you to contact us directly so the matter can be reviewed and resolved promptly.
