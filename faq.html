QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DAconnect Exclusive and how it works for your business.

What is sent
Each DAconnect Exclusive email contains a professionally presented, single-page summary of up to 14 carefully selected local businesses. This includes logos, service descriptions, and direct contact details.
Who receives the DAconnect Exclusive email
DAconnect Exclusive is issued to all relevant, publicly listed Development Application email contacts. Non-applicable government (.gov) addresses are excluded by design.
When are DAconnect Exclusive emails sent
DAconnect Exclusive emails are sent shortly after a Development Application is lodged, once the relevant public contact details are available.
How many exclusive emails are sent
We send a single email to each development-related contact address only, ensuring full compliance with Australian law.
How many businesses are included
Each DAconnect Exclusive email includes a maximum of 14 businesses.
Will I be the only business of my type
We aim to keep competition low and relevance high. While we can't always guarantee exclusivity by trade, each development is shared with a strictly limited group of businesses—never more than 14 in total.
Where will my business be placed
Business placement within each DAconnect Exclusive email is randomly ordered for every send. This ensures fairness across campaigns.
Can I cancel at any time
Yes. DAconnect operates on a cancel-anytime basis. There are no lock-in contracts.
What happens when I cancel
Once a cancellation is requested, your business will simply be removed from future DAconnect Exclusive emails. There are no penalties or additional charges.
Is there a minimum term
No. There is no minimum term required to use DAconnect.
What if I've paid annually
If you choose an annual payment option, it is offered at a discounted rate. Annual payments are non-refundable once the service has commenced.
How do I know DAconnect is working
DAconnect provides clear, tangible, and auditable proof of value. Businesses can see their details being actively presented to real Development Application contacts.
Will I receive confirmation that my business was included
Yes. Businesses receive confirmation that their details were included in DAconnect Exclusive communications.
Can I pay for better placement
No. DAconnect does not offer paid placement or prioritisation. Business placement is randomly ordered for each email to ensure fairness.
Do I need any special software or access
No. DAconnect does not require any special software, logins, or technical setup. Everything is handled for you.
Is this spam
No. DAconnect operates in full compliance with Australian communications and spam legislation. Each Development Application contact address is emailed once only.
Are my business details shared elsewhere
No. Your business details are used solely for inclusion in DAconnect Exclusive communications. Your information is not sold, shared, or used for unrelated marketing purposes.
Can I change my business details later
Yes. You can update your business details if they change by contacting the DAconnect team.
Can I be included in more than one council
Yes. Businesses can choose to be included across multiple councils, subject to availability and relevance.
Who is DAconnect best suited for
DAconnect is best suited for builders, trades, consultants, and suppliers who want early visibility on real Development Applications.
Can I approach the development contacts after DAconnect has sent my business card
Yes. Once DAconnect has introduced your business, you're free to follow up independently. DAconnect acts as the first touch—a warm, professional introduction. From there, any direct follow-up is entirely up to you.
